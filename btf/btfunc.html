Programming the Button Tracking Functionality

I have programmed the spotlights to track the mouse cursor as it hovers over the main menu buttons. Along with a nice color shifting animation.

Of course its a tricky task because of the intricacies of SVG animations and how SVG elements behave.

In order to incorporate this feature, we have to terminate our currently active animations as well as "clear" their history (so as to take out any lingering animation that might have an effect on it). We then have to assign it to a new class that is linked to a CSS variable that updates every time the mouse moves.

Then upon the mouse leaving the menu button, we have to switch the classes of the spotlights back to their original ones and once again clear their history so that they can safely restart. A diagram of the process is attached below.
